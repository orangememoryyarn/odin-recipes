Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pizza was originally invented in Naples, Italy, but has since become symbolic of modern America. Pepperoni Pizza - one of the most popular subtypes of Pizza - is my favorite.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18-20 slices of pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon pink salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper
  • flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Add toppings
  5. Cook the Pizza
