Risotto

Description

A risotto is a rice-based, northern Italian dish that often includes a mixture of herbs, chicken and vegetables

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Corn
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • Herbs
  • Broth or Stock
  • Parmesan Cheese
