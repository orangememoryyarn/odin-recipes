Sweet Corn

Description

A quick Indian snack often consumed while at the cinema, sweet corn (also known as Masala Corn) is a delicious, vegan, treat.

Ingredients

  • Black Pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Corn
  • Cilantro
  • Butter
  • Chaat Masala
  • Black Salt
  • Lemon Juice
  • Corn (duh)

Steps

  1. Rinse the corn, add it to a microwave-safe bowl, and top it with butter
  2. Microwave for 1.5 to 2 minutes, stirring every 30 seconds till the butter's melted and the corn is hot
  3. Chop the cilantro and add it to the bowl alongside the masala, peppers, salt and lemon juice
  4. Mix well and taste, adjusting the seasoning as needed
